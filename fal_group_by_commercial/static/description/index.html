Story behind the features

Odoo behavior
Odoo gives the possibility to create the orgchart of your partners.


CLuedoo behavior
You can get the hierarchical view of your order based on the orgchart of your partners, in order to get the order you did with each subsidiary of your partner and the total order you did with the group.

Module +
When you work with different entities of the same group, this hierarchical view proposes a way to get our business according to the partner organisation structure. It requires to properly configure the orgchart of your partners by filling the parent company field in partner form view.

CLuedoo recommendation
A must when you customers are big group and so that you can be contracted by any subsidiary of the group. A plus in other case.

Our Feature as Solution

Manage the ability to put parent on partner that is company

How to Use this Module?

1

Go to Contacts, create a partner with Company type, we can set parent company of this partner.
